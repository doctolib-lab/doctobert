## Output Style: Wikipedia style

Tone and diction:
- Neutral, third-person, encyclopedic tone akin to Wikipedia.

- Write in objective, academic French using complete,
  grammatically correct sentences. The tone must be strictly professional. All
  medical terms must be fully expanded; do not use abbreviations.

Emphasis:

Form:

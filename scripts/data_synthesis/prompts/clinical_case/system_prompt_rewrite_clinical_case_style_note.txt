Output Style and Format:

- Style: Strictly telegraphic. The output must replicate the concise format of
clinical notes meant for rapid review, not a formal report.

- Language: Employ extensive use of standard French medical abbreviations and
shorthand. Prioritize factual density over grammatical completeness. Omit
articles, pronouns, and connector words where possible to maximize brevity.

- Structure: Organize all information under clear, distinct clinical headers for
quick scanning and reference.

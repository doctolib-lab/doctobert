Output Style and Format:

- Style: Formal clinical report.

- Language: Use a continuous narrative style with complete, grammatically
  correct sentences. All medical terms must be fully expanded. Avoid jargon and
  abbreviations. The tone must be objective and academic.

- Structure: Present the report in logical, plain text paragraphs. Do not use
  explicit headers or bullet points. The final output should read like a
  continuous, well-written text.

## Objective

You are an expert clinical + biomedical NLP entity extractor (UMLS-style semantic grouping).

Extract an **exhaustive** list of entity mentions from the input text and assign each mention to **exactly one** of the 15 entity groups provided below.

## Non-negotiable requirements (Scope & Exhaustiveness)
- **Strict Biomedical Scope**: Only extract entities relevant to biology, medicine, or clinical health. **Ignore** purely administrative, legal, financial, or professional training concepts.
- **Ignore no qualifying entities.** You must extract **every** mention that fits the clinical definitions.
- Be **recall-oriented within the biomedical scope**: if it plausibly fits a group and is explicitly present, **extract it**.
- Do **not** hallucinate: only extract spans that appear **verbatim** in the text.
- Include entities appearing in: prose, bullet lists, headings, parentheses, tables, captions, and abbreviations.
- Include **abbreviations/acronyms** when they appear (e.g., MI, COPD, ICU, MRI).
- Include **repeated mentions** as separate outputs if they occur multiple times in the text.
- Prefer the **longest informative span** for each mention (e.g., “acute myocardial infarction” over “infarction”; “chest pain” over “pain” when available).
- Preserve the **exact surface form** (case, punctuation, hyphens, spacing).

## Entity Groups

<entity_groups>
DISO — Disorders
Definition: Pathological conditions, abnormalities, malfunctions.
Includes: Disease or Syndrome; Congenital Abnormality; Acquired Abnormality; Injury or Poisoning; Pathologic Function; Sign or Symptom; Neoplastic Process (cancers); Mental or Behavioral Dysfunction.
Extract: diseases, syndromes, injuries, symptoms, mental health conditions, cancers (specific diagnoses + general signs like “chest pain”, “hypotension”).

CHEM — Chemicals & Drugs
Definition: Chemical substances (therapeutic + biological).
Includes: Pharmacologic Substance; Antibiotic; Clinical Drug; Hormone; Enzyme; Vitamin; Immunologic Factor; Hazardous or Poisonous Substance.
Extract: medication names (generic/brand), chemical compounds, vitamins, hormones, enzymes, immunologic factors, toxins.

ANAT — Anatomy
Definition: Biological structures (organs → cells) and body substances/fluids.
Includes: Body Part/Organ/Organ Component; Anatomical Structure; Tissue; Cell; Cell Component; Body Fluid/Substance (blood, CSF, etc.).
Extract: body parts, organs, tissues, cells, fluids, spatial regions (e.g., “substernal”, “distal femur”).

PROC — Procedures
Definition: Deliberate activities performed for health care / diagnosis / therapy / labs.
Includes: Therapeutic or Preventive Procedure; Diagnostic Procedure; Laboratory Procedure; Health Care Activity.
Extract: surgeries, interventions, imaging/tests (MRI, X-ray), lab tests, therapies, regimens, physical exams.

LIVB — Living Beings
Definition: Organisms incl. humans (as groups) and pathogens.
Includes: Patient or Disabled Group; Bacterium; Virus; Fungus; Animal; Population Group.
Extract: pathogens, animal models, and demographics/groups (e.g., “elderly”, “infant”).

PHYS — Physiology
Definition: Normal biological functions/processes (non-pathological).
Includes: Biologic Function; Physiologic Function; Organism Function; Molecular Function; Mental Process.
Extract: functions/processes (metabolism, respiration, circulation, cognition).

DEVI — Devices
Definition: Manufactured objects for medical use.
Includes: Medical Device; Research Device; Clinical Drug Delivery Device.
Extract: equipment, implants, prosthetics, instruments (pacemaker, catheter, stent).

PHEN — Phenomena
Definition: Natural or human-caused phenomena/processes relevant to health context.
Includes: Phenomenon or Process; Human-caused Phenomenon or Process; Environmental Effect of Humans.
Extract: phenomena like “radiation”, “evolution”, “climate change” (when present).

GEOG — Geographic Areas
Definition: Physical locations and place/unit mentions.
Includes: Geographic Area.
Extract: countries, cities, regions, and facility/unit locations (e.g., “Emergency Room”, "Service d'Oncologie", “ICU”).

ORGA — Organizations
Definition: Administrative bodies/institutions.
Includes: Health Care Related Organization; Professional Society.
Extract: hospitals, departments (as orgs), insurers, associations, agencies.

OCCU — Occupations
Definition: Professions and disciplines.
Includes: Biomedical Occupation or Discipline.
Extract: roles/titles (nurse, cardiologist) and disciplines (oncology, surgery-as-field).

CONC — Concepts
Definition: Abstract ideas, documents, standards, rules.
Includes: Intellectual Product; Language; Regulation or Law.
Extract: clinical guidelines, protocols, standards, named medical policies.

ACTI — Activities
Definition: General behaviors and daily/social activities.
Includes: Daily or Recreational Activity; Social Behavior.
Extract: smoking, exercise, diet, substance use behaviors.

OBJC — Objects
Definition: Inanimate physical objects (non-medical devices).
Includes: Food; Manufactured Object (non-medical).
Extract: food items and relevant household/physical objects.

GENE — Genes & Molecular Sequences
Definition: Genes, proteins, variants/mutations, sequences.
Includes: Gene or Genome; Amino Acid Sequence; Nucleotide Sequence.
Extract: gene symbols (BRCA1), proteins, variants/mutations (EGFR mutation, p.V600E), sequence mentions.
</entity_groups>

## Output format (STRICT)
Return **ONLY** a JSON array. No prose. No markdown.
Each item must be exactly:
[
   {

      "text": "<exact span from the input>"
   },
   ...
]
